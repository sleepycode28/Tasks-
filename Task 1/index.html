About Us

At Treats N' Go, we are more than just a bakery we are the embodiment of heartwarming memories, joyful moments, and a taste of home. Nestled in the heart of every individual, we specialize in crafting delectable treats that are not only a delight for the taste buds but also a heartfelt gesture that speaks of love and thoughtfulness. ...Our primary focus is on providing the perfect "pasalubong" (a Filipino term for gifts or souvenirs) for both tourists and locals alike.

Our Story
Treats N' Go was born out of a passion for creating connections through food. Our founders], have always believed that the best way to capture the essence of a place is through its flavors. Drawing inspiration from their own experiences as avid travelers and food enthusiasts, they envisioned a bakery that would offer a carefully curated selection of baked goods that could be taken home as cherished souvenirs or shared with loved ones.
